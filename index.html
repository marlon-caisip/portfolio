Who am I

I'm Marlon Caisip, I've designed applications, and websites that are more simpler and easy to use, lessening the friction to the users. I have spent time learning and practicing my skills in many fields.

My Track Record

HTML, CSS, and JavaScript are some of my technical skills. I have background knowledge in Java, Python, SQL, and React. I also took training and seminars in User Experience Design, User Journey Mapping, and UI/UX design. I have experience in drawing cartoon and manga figures and using Adobe products like Premiere Pro, After Effects, Audition, and Photoshop.

Designing has always been a passion of mine. I graduated from Soledad R. Villafuerte Elementary School, finished my Junior High School at CBSUA - Sipocot, and Senior High School at King Thomas Learning Academy Inc. I am currently taking a bachelor's degree in Information Technology at CBSUA Sipocot.

What I do

I make simple yet functional designs that make people's lives easier such as application, website, and product design. I dislike too much visual clutter, it can be distracting, so I make it direct and honest through user-first design principles that is both visually appealing and easy to understand. For me, designing is an endless process of trying new approach that is beneficial to users experience, as well as finding out what's pleasing to eyes yet meaningful and useful, and solving problems that matters.
